# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or edit content and may ban contributors for behavior
that violates this code.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces and project-related communication channels.
